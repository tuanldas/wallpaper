# Wallpaper Categories & Templates

## Description
The app organizes wallpapers into 11 categories, displayed as horizontally scrollable tabs. Each category contains multiple templates with distinct styles. Users can filter wallpapers by "All" or "Favorite".

## Access Location
Home screen > Tabs bar (below the "Create wallpaper" banner)

## Category List

### 1. For you
- **Description**: Curated wallpapers based on user preferences (selected during onboarding)
- **Style**: Diverse - nature, kawaii, anime, quote, hearts, live wallpapers
- **Count**: 20+ templates (infinite scroll, loads more on scroll)
- **Characteristics**: Default tab when opening the app, mixes all styles
- **Screenshot**: `01_for_you/screenshots/`

### 2. DIY
- **Description**: Do-it-yourself style wallpapers, collages, scrapbooks
- **Style**: Photo collage/polaroid, anime girl illustrations, photo compositions, bows/ribbons
- **Count**: 15+ templates
- **Characteristics**: Many wallpapers feature collage-style layouts with multiple combined images
- **Screenshot**: `02_diy/screenshots/`

### 3. Love
- **Description**: Love and heart-themed wallpapers
- **Style**: Hearts (various styles), neon, 3D, flowers, crystals, couples
- **Count**: 12+ templates
- **Characteristics**: Many live wallpapers, predominantly pink/red tones
- **Screenshot**: `03_love/screenshots/`

### 4. Live
- **Description**: Animated live wallpapers
- **Style**: Landscapes (mountains, sunsets), anime scenery, holographic/aurora, fire
- **Count**: 10+ templates
- **Characteristics**: All have a "Live" badge, animated wallpapers with motion effects
- **Screenshot**: `04_live/screenshots/`

### 5. Lock Screen
- **Description**: Wallpapers specifically designed for the lock screen
- **Style**: Anime scenery, illustration, doodles, neon cityscape, gradient
- **Count**: 10+ templates
- **Characteristics**: Optimized for display with clock and notifications
- **Screenshot**: `05_lock_screen/screenshots/`

### 6. Kawaii
- **Description**: Japanese-style cute wallpapers
- **Style**: Cute animals, ice cream trucks, picnic, pastel colors, illustrations
- **Count**: 8+ templates
- **Characteristics**: Pastel color palette, cute characters, hand-drawn style
- **Screenshot**: `06_kawaii/screenshots/`

### 7. Aesthetic
- **Description**: Aesthetic and dreamy wallpapers
- **Style**: Tulips, pink moon, sunset, gradient, fairy lights
- **Count**: 8+ templates
- **Characteristics**: Pastel pink/purple tones, dreamy style
- **Screenshot**: `07_aesthetic/screenshots/`

### 8. Quote
- **Description**: Wallpapers featuring inspirational quotes
- **Style**: Typography on floral backgrounds, gradient, minimalist
- **Count**: 8+ templates
- **Example quotes**: "BE THE BEST VERSION OF YOU", "it girl", "Fear Reaction Courage Decision"
- **Characteristics**: Beautiful typography combined with background imagery
- **Screenshot**: `08_quote/screenshots/`

### 9. Famous
- **Description**: Celebrity and superhero wallpapers
- **Style**: Pop art, digital painting, artistic photography
- **Count**: 6+ templates
- **Examples**: Cristiano Ronaldo, Spider-Man, Messi (pop art style)
- **Characteristics**: Primarily sports and superhero themes
- **Screenshot**: `09_famous/screenshots/`

### 10. Car

- **Style**: Supercars, trucks, professional photography and digital art
- **Count**: 6+ templates

- **Characteristics**: Vibrant colors, impressive angles

### 11. Anime
- **Description**: Anime/manga wallpapers
- **Style**: Anime scenery, female anime characters, magic, cherry blossoms
- **Count**: 6+ templates
- **Characteristics**: Many live wallpapers, Japanese art style
- **Screenshot**: `11_anime/screenshots/`

## Filter Feature
- **Location**: "Filter by" / "All" dropdown in the top-right corner of the wallpaper grid
- **Options**:
  - **All**: Displays all wallpapers in the category
  - **Favorite**: Shows only wallpapers marked as favorites (heart icon)

## General UI/UX
- **Tabs bar**: Horizontally scrollable, active tab has a rounded pink border
- **Each tab**: Has a small icon to the left of the text
- **Wallpaper grid**: 3 columns, each wallpaper includes:
  - Thumbnail preview
  - Heart/favorite button (top-right corner)
  - "Live" badge for live wallpapers
- **Ads**: Native ads interspersed within the wallpaper grid
- **Infinite scroll**: Loads more templates when scrolling down

## Notes
- Categories are ordered left to right: For you > DIY > Love > Live > Lock Screen > Kawaii > Aesthetic > Quote > Famous > Car > Anime
- "For you" is the default tab, displaying content based on preferences selected during onboarding
- Many wallpapers appear across multiple categories
- Live wallpapers can appear in any category, not just the "Live" tab
